First Aid Guide

Quick emergency instructions until professional medical help arrives.

Snake Bite

🔴 Critical Emergency

A venomous snake bite can become life-threatening. Keep the victim calm and seek immediate medical assistance.

Steps to Follow

  1. Move away from the snake safely.
  2. Keep the victim calm and still.
  3. Immobilize the bitten limb.
  4. Remove rings or tight clothing.
  5. Call emergency services immediately.

Do NOT

  • Cut the wound.
  • Suck out the venom.
  • Apply ice.
  • Use a tourniquet.

Burns

🟠 High Priority

Cooling a burn quickly helps reduce tissue damage.

Steps to Follow

  1. Remove the person from the heat source.
  2. Cool the burn under running water for 20 minutes.
  3. Remove jewelry before swelling starts.
  4. Cover with a clean dressing.
  5. Seek medical care if severe.

Do NOT

  • Apply butter.
  • Use toothpaste.
  • Burst blisters.

Severe Bleeding

🔴 Critical Emergency

Heavy bleeding must be controlled immediately to prevent shock.

Steps to Follow

  1. Apply direct pressure.
  2. Keep pressure constant.
  3. Raise the limb if possible.
  4. Add more dressing if needed.
  5. Call emergency services.

Do NOT

  • Remove soaked bandages.
  • Wash a heavily bleeding wound.

Choking

🔴 Critical Emergency

A blocked airway can become fatal within minutes.

Steps to Follow

  1. Encourage coughing.
  2. Give 5 back blows.
  3. Give 5 abdominal thrusts.
  4. Repeat until the object comes out.
  5. Start CPR if unconscious.

Do NOT

  • Give food or water.
  • Blindly put fingers into the mouth.

Broken Bone

🟠 High Priority

Minimize movement to prevent further injury.

Steps to Follow

  1. Keep the injured area still.
  2. Support the limb.
  3. Apply a cold pack.
  4. Use a splint if trained.
  5. Seek medical attention.

Do NOT

  • Straighten the bone.
  • Move the victim unnecessarily.
